About Us

BRIEF HISTORY

Kien San Group first started out as Kien San Metal Sdn Bhd, a small metal recycling centre at Pending, Kuching, which was founded and managed by the late Mr. Hong Kian Sing in 1999 together with a few partners, Tan Sri Dato’ Pheng Yin Huah, Dato’ Hong Thian Hock and Datin Hong Cheng Guat.


Kien San Metal Sdn Bhd was the pioneer of scrap metal recycling in the East Malaysia region; Kien San Metal based our operations on integrity, honesty and versatility towards our customers’ needs; we have grown from a small scale scrap metal recycler into Kien San Group which spans across various locations in East Malaysia from Kuching to Kota Kinabalu as well as Johor Bahru in Peninsular Malaysia.


Kien San Group is currently the leading integrated scrap recycling in the industry and one of the most trusted and recognized name in the industry.

VISION

We are always striving for the best and we are constantly working towards zero wastage and maximising recycling rates at our well-equipped plants across the East Malaysian region. We endeavour to maintain our position at the forefront of the scrap metal recycling industry by versatility and integrity whether you are selling to us or buying from us.


We at  Kien San Group are also  dedicated  to  the  significantly  reducing  the environmental impact caused by the steel and manufacturing industries. The resourceful recycling of scrap metal by Kien San Group allows metals to be reused in the manufacturing industry resulting in the reduction of the consumption of our natural resources and helps conserve our natural resources.
