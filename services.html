Services

OUR SERVICES

Kien San Group offers a full range of comprehensive metal recycling solutions ranging from scrap-metal collections to industrial scrapping of plants and factories to ship breaking services to meet our customers’ demands and needs.


We deal all kinds of ferrous and non-ferrous metals; including;


  1. FERROUS & NON-FERROUS PAPERS & METAL

    Scrap Iron, Scrap Cars, Cast Iron, High Carbon, Scrap Rail, Copper, Bronze, Brass, Aluminium, Alloy, Stainless Steel, Batteries, Lead, Empty Iron Drum and Miscellaneous Metals.

  2. MACHINERY/PUMPS

    All types of Used Machineries, Mechanical Pumps, Electrical Pumps, Cooling Towers and Compressors.

  3. OFFICE EQUIPMENTS

    Computers, Printers, Air-condition Units, Computer accessories, Steel Cabinets.

  4. ELECTRICAL ITEMS

    Cables and Electrical wires.

  5. HOUSEHOLD EQUIPMENTS

    Kitchen Equipment, Fridge, Cookers, Ovens, Metal Cabinets.

  6. PAPERS & CARDBOARDS (Batu Kawah Branch)

    All kinds of Paper and Cardboards.

  7. DISMANTLING & DECOMMISSION WORKS

    Buildings, Factories and Warehouses.


We are also licensed by Department of Environment to collect and store Used Batteries for recovery.


We also undertake projects such as dismantling, demolition, scraping and recovery of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap from factories, buildings and construction sites, used machineries and vehicles.


We at Kien San Group are always endeavour to achieve, if not surpass the industry standards; we have always pride ourselves on surpassing our customers’ demanding standards in both quality and costs.
